Principal's Message

Welcome to Westfield Academy, where we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also character, creativity, and compassion. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to create an environment where every student can thrive.

We understand that education is not merely about textbooks and exams, but about preparing young minds for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow's world. At Westfield, we combine rigorous academics with holistic development to ensure our students graduate as well-rounded individuals.

Admissions

Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with all required information about the student and family.

Application Fee: $75 (non-refundable)

2

Document Submission

Submit all required documents, including academic records, recommendation letters, and identification.

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records (2 years)
  • Teacher recommendations (2)
  • Immunization records
  • Photo ID of parents/guardians
3

Entrance Assessment

Students will complete age-appropriate assessments to determine academic readiness and placement.

Assessment areas include Mathematics, English, and general aptitude.

4

Family Interview

Parents and students will meet with the admissions team to discuss educational goals, expectations, and ensure alignment with the school's values and mission.

5

Admission Decision

Families will be notified of the admission decision within 2-3 weeks after completing all steps.

Upon acceptance, an enrollment contract and deposit will be required to secure the student's place.

Key Dates

  • Application Opens October 1, 2025
  • Priority Deadline January 15, 2026
  • Regular Deadline March 31, 2026
  • Decision Notifications April 15, 2026
  • Enrollment Deadline May 1, 2026
